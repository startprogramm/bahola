# Eid Mubarak! 🌙

As the month of Ramadan comes to an end, we at TezTekshir want to pause and say something simple: **Eid Mubarak to every one of you.**

## To Our Teachers

You are the heart of everything we build. Day after day, you show up for your students — grading, guiding, and inspiring them. During Ramadan, many of you did all this while fasting. You did it with grace and care.

We see you, and we are grateful.

May this Eid bring you rest, joy, and time with the people you love most. You have earned it.

## To Our Students

You have worked hard this year. Whether you are submitting work, reading AI feedback, or getting ready for exams — your effort matters. Education is a gift you are giving to your future self.

Enjoy this holiday fully. Come back to your books feeling fresh.

## To Our School Communities

TezTekshir exists because schools and communities trusted us with a small piece of their work. The Presidential Schools of Uzbekistan show what it looks like to chase excellence with sincerity. We are honored to serve you.

## A Note of Thanks

Eid is a time for gratitude, forgiveness, and being together. As a team, we are grateful that what started as a simple idea — *what if AI could help teachers grade faster?* — has grown into something that genuinely helps people.

We are still learning, still growing, still building. But today, we stop to celebrate with you.

**Eid Mubarak. May your homes be full of warmth, your tables full of food, and your hearts full of peace.**
