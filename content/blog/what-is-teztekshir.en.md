# What is TezTekshir?

Grading takes too long. TezTekshir gives teachers their time back.

It uses AI to read student work, check it against your answer key, and give feedback — per question — in under three minutes.

No robots replacing teachers. No mystery about how it works. Just a fast, honest tool that handles the boring parts of checking papers so you can focus on your students.

## The Problem We're Solving

In Uzbekistan, a typical teacher grades 30–40 student papers after each test. Do that across five classes and a few tests a month — and you have teachers spending whole evenings just writing scores and notes that students barely glance at.

TezTekshir fixes this. Upload your answer key once. Students take photos of their work. Every paper is graded in minutes, with written feedback in the student's own language.

## How It Works in Three Steps

**1. You upload your answer key.**
Paste text, or upload a PDF, Word doc, Excel sheet, or a photo. TezTekshir reads it and saves your grading rules.

**2. Students submit their work.**
They photograph their written answers and upload from any browser. No app needed.

**3. AI grades, you review.**
TezTekshir reads the handwriting, compares it to your answer key, and gives a score and feedback for each question. You see results for the whole class at once. If anything looks off, you fix it with one click.

## Who Is It For?

TezTekshir is built for:

- **Teachers** who run written tests often
- **School directors** who want to see how students are doing across the school
- **Presidential Schools** in Uzbekistan, where quality and speed both matter

The platform works in **Uzbek, Russian, and English** — both for the app and for student feedback.

## What Makes It Different

Most AI grading tools only work for multiple-choice tests. TezTekshir works with **handwritten, open-answer work** — the kind students do in most Uzbek schools.

The AI doesn't just match words. It understands what the student means. So a student who explains the right idea in their own words still gets full marks. And teachers can always change any score and explain why.
