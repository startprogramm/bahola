## Production Notes

- **Montage energy:** Fast cuts but not chaotic. Each shot should land clearly before the next. 0.5–1 sec per shot in the montage section.
- **End card hint:** "2-semestr yangiliklari kelmoqda" should feel like a genuine tease — not vague corporate speak. If there's a specific feature to hint at, coordinate with product team to include a blurred/silhouetted UI peek.
- **Music:** Starts punchy (hook attention), transitions to something warmer and more hopeful for the end card. Arc: tension → momentum → optimism.
- **Classrooms:** Use real school footage if available. Diversity of subjects and schools strengthens the "this is everywhere" narrative.
- **Text timing:** "Bu faqat boshlangich." should land when the music pauses — the silence gives it weight.
- **Follow CTA:** Point to the camera or use subtle UI arrow animation toward the follow button area.
