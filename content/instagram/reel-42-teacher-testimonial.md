## Production Notes

- **Casting:** Find a willing teacher — ideally someone who already uses Bahola and can speak naturally. Authenticity over polish. A slightly imperfect delivery reads as more genuine than a rehearsed one.
- **Setting:** Real classroom or teacher's desk — not a studio. Natural light preferred. Avoid anything that looks staged.
- **B-roll:** Use actual Bahola UI screen recordings. OCR text recognition animation is the strongest visual — show it working in real-time.
- **Music:** Understated. The VO carries this reel. Music should support, not compete. Fade in at shot 4, full under end card.
- **Subtitles:** Always on. Large enough to read on mobile. Key phrases in bold.
- **End card:** "Bepul" (free) is a strong closer — lead with it.
- **Duration target:** Aim for 35 seconds. Tight but not rushed.
