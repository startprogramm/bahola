# Reel 28 — First Week Speed

## Hook (0–2s)
Stack of student papers lands on a teacher's desk with a thud. Text overlays instantly: **"1-hafta. 30 o'quvchi. 0 soat baholash."** / **"Неделя 1. 30 учеников. 0 часов проверки."**

## Visual Sequence (2–28s)

- **2–5s:** Wide shot — teacher's desk piled with first-week assignments. Teacher picks up phone, opens Bahola. Energy is fast, purposeful. **On-screen text:** *"O'quvchilar ishlari tayyor."* / *"Работы готовы."*
- **5–10s:** Time-lapse: teacher photographs papers one by one — each snap triggers an instant OCR animation on screen. Text extracts, scores populate in rapid succession. **On-screen text:** *"Suratga ol → Natija."* / *"Фото → Результат."* Each grade badge flashes: 18/20, 15/20, 19/20…
- **10–16s:** Rapid-fire montage — grade cards stacking up on screen, names blurred. A progress bar fills: "30/30 baholandi ✓" / "30/30 проверено ✓". Timer in corner: 00:47. **On-screen text:** *"47 daqiqa."* / *"47 минут."*
- **16–21s:** Teacher leans back in chair, stretches, glances at clock — 4:00 PM. Relaxed half-smile. **On-screen text:** *"Birinchi hafta. Ish tugadi."* / *"Первая неделя. Готово."*
- **21–26s:** Screen shows Bahola dashboard — all 30 submissions graded, class average displayed. Teacher taps "Export" — results sent. **On-screen text:** *"Bepul. Hoziroq sinab ko'ring."* / *"Бесплатно. Попробуйте сейчас."*
- **26–28s:** Bahola logo. Tagline: *"Tez. Aniq. Bepul."* / *"Быстро. Точно. Бесплатно."*

## Audio
Upbeat, punchy electronic — fast tempo that matches the time-lapse energy. Rhythm syncs with each snap/grade flash. No lyrics. Volume: 60% throughout, drops slightly under voiceover.

## Duration
~28 seconds

## Notes
- The time-lapse sequence is the visual anchor — make each photo-snap satisfying with a light shutter sound effect.
- Score badges should be realistic but blurred enough to avoid any real student identification.
- The "47 minutes" timer is the key stat — keep it on screen long enough to register.
- Clock at 4:00 PM reinforces the "done early" feeling without being heavy-handed.
- Teacher's relaxed lean-back is the emotional beat — don't rush past it.
- Show real Bahola UI; avoid showing student names in close-ups.
