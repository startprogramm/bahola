# Reel 14 — Graduation Moment

**Filename:** `reel-14-graduation-moment.md`
**Campaign:** Week 7 — Graduation Season & New School Outreach
**Duration:** ~30 seconds
**Format:** Emotion-forward montage
**Language:** Uzbek (overlays)

---

## Concept

A teacher approaching exam season — not with dread, but with calm confidence. The reel traces a simple arc: stress → action → relief. Photo upload, AI feedback, done. The emotional payoff is in the final beat: the teacher looks at results and smiles. Built for end-of-year when teachers feel the weight of final grading the most.

---

## Hook (First 3 Seconds)

> **"Endi imtihon mavsumi qo'rqinchli emas."**
> ("Exam season isn't scary anymore.")

Teacher's face — calm, slight smile — looking at phone. Cut to close-up of the screen showing graded results.

## Production Notes

- Cast a teacher who looks genuinely relaxed — not performing relief, actually calm
- The stack of papers on the desk is intentional: the contrast is "papers exist, but they don't own you anymore"
- Bahola UI should show real feedback in Uzbek — not a mock
- Aspect ratio: 9:16 (vertical Reels format)
- Keep the cut pace slow and warm — this is emotional, not kinetic
- Redact any student names in grading results
