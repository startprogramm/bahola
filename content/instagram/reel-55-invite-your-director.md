# Reel 55: Invite Your Director — Bring Your Whole School Into Spring

**Slug:** `reel-55-invite-your-director`
**Publish date:** December 10, 2026 (Thursday — Winter Break & Onboarding week)
**Duration:** 30–40 seconds

---

## Concept

A single teacher's journey from solo user to school-wide champion. They've been using TezTekshir alone. Now they realize: the director should know about this. The reel walks through the insight and the simple act of sharing — a message, a link, a conversation. Tone: empowering, light, and slightly conspiratorial. "You know something your school doesn't yet. Here's how to share it."

Audience: TezTekshir teachers who are using the platform but haven't yet brought in leadership. This is the nudge to become an internal advocate.

---

## Hook

**On-screen text (first 2 seconds):**
> **"Siz bilasiz. Direktoringiz bilmaydi."**

## Production Notes

- **The hook is the tension.** "Siz bilasiz. Direktoringiz bilmaydi." creates a gap between what the teacher knows and what the school knows. This is the emotional engine of the reel. Don't resolve it too fast — let it sit for a beat.
- **Shot 3 is the key action.** The moment of sending the link to the director is the emotional climax. Make it visible: show the message being typed, the link pasted, the send button tapped. This is what we want viewers to copy.
- **Do NOT show the director's reaction.** We don't have that footage, and faking it looks staged. The outcome is implied by the school hallway shot (Shot 4). Let the audience's imagination fill the gap.
- **Caption tone is peer-level, not preachy.** "Siz bilasiz" (You know) is a compliment — it positions the teacher as the smart one in the room. This should feel like insider knowledge being passed on, not a corporate pitch.
- **Keep it under 38 seconds.** This reel has a clear narrative arc (discovery → action → outcome). Don't pad it. Every second should serve the story.
- **The CTA is a share action, not a signup.** "Direktoringizga ulashing" (Share with your director) — the call-to-action is about advocacy, not self-registration. This reel is targeting existing users who haven't yet recruited their leadership.
- **Bilingual caption.** Uzbek first, Russian second. Both should feel personal and direct.
- **Publish Thursday (Dec 10), not Monday.** Give reel-54 (prep/checklist) space to breathe first. This reel has a different energy and needs its own moment.
