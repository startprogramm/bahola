# Reel 10 — Subject Deep Dive: AI Grades a Math Problem

**Filename:** `reel-10-subject-deep-dive.md`
**Campaign:** Week 5 — Back-to-School Post-Eid
**Duration:** 30–45 seconds
**Format:** Screen capture / product demo with text overlays
**Language:** Uzbek (overlays + caption)

---

## Concept

Show TezTekshir grading a handwritten math worksheet in real-time. The viewer watches the full journey — messy student handwriting → AI recognition → instant grade and feedback — in under 45 seconds.

---
