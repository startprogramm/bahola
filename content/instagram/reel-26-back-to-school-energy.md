# Reel 26 — Back to School Energy

## Hook (0–2s)
Text slams onto screen over black: **"Yangi o'quv yili — yangi usul."** / **"Новый учебный год — новый способ."** Cut hard to action.

## Visual Sequence (2–22s)

- **2–5s:** Teacher sits at desk, buried in a tall stack of paper worksheets. Tired expression. Clock on wall shows 7:00 PM. **On-screen text:** *"Ilgari..."* / *"Раньше..."*
- **5–8s:** Hard cut. Same teacher — same desk, but now holding a phone. Stack of papers replaced by a single notebook. Clock shows 4:30 PM. Bright expression. **On-screen text:** *"Endi..."* / *"Теперь..."*
- **8–12s:** Close-up: teacher photographs a student's handwritten answer sheet with the phone. Quick zoom on the Bahola app UI — shutter animation, then OCR text populates instantly.
- **12–16s:** Grade result appears on screen — score, breakdown, and AI-generated feedback in Uzbek. **On-screen text:** *"Soniyalar ichida natija."* / *"Результат за секунды."*
- **16–20s:** Split screen — left side: old method (red pen, eraser, timer ticking). Right side: Bahola (phone, instant result, teacher smiling). **On-screen text:** *"Tanlov sizniki."* / *"Выбор за вами."*
- **20–22s:** Bahola logo + text: *"Bepul. Hoziroq boshlang."* / *"Бесплатно. Начните сейчас."*

## Audio
Upbeat, punchy lo-fi hip-hop — confident and energetic but not chaotic. Beat drops at the split-screen moment (16s). Volume: 70% throughout.

## Duration
~22 seconds

## Notes
- Fast cuts on beats — montage energy, not talking-head pacing.
- Stack of papers visual is key — teachers will instantly relate.
- Split screen at 16s is the hero moment — make it visually satisfying.
- Show actual Bahola app UI during OCR/grading sequences.
- No fake student names or real grades visible in UI shots.
