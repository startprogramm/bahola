## Script / Shot List

**[0:00–0:03] Hook — Text overlay on black**
- Text: "Sinov ishlarini baholash endi 20 daqiqa ⏱️"
- Cut to classroom, teacher collecting student papers

**[0:03–0:08] Scene 1 — Collect**
- Teacher walks down aisle, students hand in test sheets
- Text overlay: "1. Ishlarni yig'ing"
- Fast cut, upbeat music kicks in

**[0:08–0:15] Scene 2 — Photograph**
- Teacher places papers on desk, opens phone camera
- Photographs each sheet — fast montage (3–4 quick cuts)
- Text overlay: "2. Rasmga oling"
- Sound: camera shutter click

**[0:15–0:22] Scene 3 — Upload**
- Screen recording: teacher opens Bahola app, selects class, taps "Upload"
- Photos load into submission screen
- Text overlay: "3. Bahola'ga yuklang"

**[0:22–0:30] Scene 4 — AI Grades**
- Screen recording: progress bar / "AI grading..." animation
- Results appear: student names, scores, individual feedback
- Text overlay: "4. AI baholaydi — sekundalarda!"
- Zoom in on score breakdown screen

**[0:30–0:38] Scene 5 — Results / Payoff**
- Teacher smiles, leans back, holds up phone showing results
- Text overlay: "Natijalar tayyor. Dars davom etadi."
- Cut to wide classroom shot — teacher back to teaching, not marking

**[0:38–0:42] CTA Slate**
- Bahola logo on white background
- Text: "Bepul boshlang → bahola.uz"
