# Reel 29 — Student Feedback Moment

## Hook (0–2s)
Close-up on a student's face — wide eyes, surprised half-smile as they read feedback on a screen. Text overlays: **"Bu... mening ishim haqida?"** / **"Это... про мою работу?"**

## Visual Sequence (2–28s)

- **2–6s:** Classroom setting. Teacher hands student a phone showing TezTekshir feedback screen. Student leans in, reads. Camera stays on student's expression — genuine curiosity, then recognition. **On-screen text:** *"Birinchi topshiriq. Birinchi fikr-mulohaza."* / *"Первое задание. Первая обратная связь."*
- **6–11s:** Phone screen close-up — AI feedback visible (blurred names): detailed comment per question, what was correct, what to improve. Score badge: 16/20. Highlight animation on the "Nima yaxshi qilding" / "Что сделал хорошо" section. **On-screen text:** *"Shaxsiy. Batafsil. Tezkor."* / *"Личная. Подробная. Быстрая."*
- **11–16s:** Student scrolls down to "Keyingi safar nima qilish kerak" / "Что улучшить в следующий раз" section. Reaction: slight nod — "aah, I get it." Close-up on student's hand pointing at specific feedback line. **On-screen text:** *"O'quvchi biladi — qayerda o'sishi kerak."* / *"Ученик знает — где расти."*
- **16–21s:** Cut to teacher watching from across the room — quiet satisfaction. No words needed. The student is engaged, learning, asking a follow-up question. **On-screen text:** *"Bu baholash emas — bu o'rganish."* / *"Это не оценка — это обучение."*
- **21–26s:** Student looks up at teacher with a nod — appreciation. Teacher gives a small thumbs-up. Quick cut to TezTekshir feedback screen again: all 28 students, individual feedback loaded. **On-screen text:** *"28 o'quvchi. Har biri — shaxsiy fikr-mulohaza."* / *"28 учеников. Каждый — личная обратная связь."*
- **26–28s:** TezTekshir logo. Tagline: *"Tez. Aniq. Bepul."* / *"Быстро. Точно. Бесплатно."*

## Audio
Soft, warm piano with gentle build — understated and emotional. Matches the "quiet learning moment" energy. Volume: 45% throughout, slight swell at the teacher-student nod moment (16–21s). No heavy beats — this is an intimate scene, not a time-lapse.

## Duration
~28 seconds

## Notes
- The student's face is the hero of this reel — their genuine reaction to reading personal feedback is the hook. Cast/direct carefully.
- Do NOT rush the 11–16s scroll section. Let the viewer actually read a line of feedback before moving on — that realness sells it.
- The teacher watching quietly from across the room (16–21s) is the emotional anchor for teachers watching this reel. Hold it 1 second longer than feels comfortable.
- Feedback text must be clearly AI-generated (structured, thorough) but all student names and identifying info must be blurred.
- The final stat "28 o'quvchi — har biri shaxsiy fikr-mulohaza" is the payoff for teacher viewers — make it land clearly.
- Show real TezTekshir UI. Blurred names, visible feedback structure.
