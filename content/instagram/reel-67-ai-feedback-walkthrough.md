## Music Direction
Warm, human, inspiring. Not upbeat productivity energy — more storytelling/testimonial warmth. The teacher testimonial moment (41-46s) should feel genuine and quiet. Build slightly toward the end for the CTA. Think: documentary-style teacher profile reel energy.

## Production Notes
- The AI feedback text must be readable on mobile — use real TezTekshir UI or clean mockup
- The feedback bullets are the hero content of this reel — make them large and clearly legible
- The teacher quote (41-46s) should feel authentic — not scripted-sounding. Slow down here.
- The 3-subject montage (47-52s) should be fast — 1s per subject, just to show breadth
- The fictional teacher name "Nilufar Rashidova" can be replaced with a real teacher if available
- Uzbek captions throughout; offer Russian version for wider reach
- Consider a "Save this" CTA — teachers who are skeptical about AI quality will save this for later review
