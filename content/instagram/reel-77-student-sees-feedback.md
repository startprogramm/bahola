# Reel 77 — Student Reads the Feedback (40 Seconds)

## Concept
First-person student POV: they submitted their work, got graded, and now see not just a score but a detailed explanation of what they did well and what to improve. The reel shows the emotional journey — anticipation, reading, understanding, motivation. Targets teachers who want to convey that AI grading helps students learn, not just be scored.

## Hook (0–5s)
Student at desk, phone in hand. Notification appears: "Sizning natijangiz tayyor ✅"
Bold overlay: **"Baho — faqat raqam emas."**
Subtext (RU): **"Оценка — это не просто цифра."**

## Scene Breakdown

**[0–6s] — Kutish / Ожидание**
- Student submits photos of work in TezTekshir app — upload animation
- Overlay (UZ): "Topshirdi. Kutmoqda."
- Overlay (RU): "Отправил. Ждёт."
- Quick time-lapse effect — phone notification arrives

**[6–16s] — Natija / Результат**
- Student opens result page — score displays with animation: "16/20"
- Student expression: curious, not anxious
- Screen shows AI feedback sections: "To'g'ri javoblar" / "Xatolar" / "Tavsiya"
- Overlay (UZ): "Nima to'g'ri ✅"
- Overlay (UZ): "Nima xato ❌"
- Overlay (UZ): "Keyingi safar nima qilish kerak 💡"
- Overlay (RU): "Что правильно / Где ошибка / Что улучшить"

**[16–28s] — Tushunish / Понимание**
- Student reading carefully — finger traces text on screen
- Specific AI feedback line highlighted (anonymized): "4-savolda formula to'g'ri, lekin hisob noto'g'ri"
- Student writes a note in notebook: the correction
- Overlay (UZ): "Xato topildi — tuzatish boshlandi"
- Overlay (RU): "Ошибка найдена — исправление начато"

**[28–36s] — Natija: rivojlanish / Рост**
- Side-by-side: first submission score "12/20" → latest "16/20"
- Overlay (UZ): "Har topshiriq — yangi dars"
- Overlay (RU): "Каждая работа — новый урок"
- Student smiles — not embarrassed by the score, motivated by the feedback

**[36–40s] — End card**
- Bold: **"TezTekshir — o'quvchi uchun ham"**
- Subtext (RU): "TezTekshir — и для ученика тоже"
- TezTekshir logo + "teztekshir.uz"
