## Music Direction
Upbeat, energetic comparison energy. The kind of music used in "life hack" or "productivity hack" reels. Clear audio break when switching from LEFT (frustrated/slow) to RIGHT (efficient/happy). Builds momentum during the grading animation sequence.

## Production Notes
- The split screen must be very clean and readable — label each side clearly from frame 1
- Use a real-time counter (like a stopwatch) that's visible in both halves — this is the key visual proof element
- LEFT side: desaturated / cooler tones to feel tired and slow. RIGHT side: warm, bright, energetic.
- The 90% time saving stat should be big and bold — it's the key takeaway number
- Consider a "Save this" CTA — teachers will share this with colleagues
- Captions in Uzbek throughout; Russian subtitles optional
- Keep the comparison fair: same class size (30 students), same subject context
