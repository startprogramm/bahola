# Reel 14 — School's End-of-Semester Transformation Story

**Filename:** `reel-14-semester-close-story.md`
**Campaign:** Week 6 — End-of-Semester Push (2026-04-10)
**Duration:** 30–40 seconds
**Format:** Before/after narrative with text overlays, photo montage style
**Language:** Uzbek (overlays) + English hashtags

---

## Concept

A "before and after" story following a teacher through end-of-semester chaos vs. calm. Split-screen or sequential: old way (stack of papers, late nights) vs. TezTekshir way (phone camera, results in minutes). Relatable emotional arc — exhaustion transformed into confidence.

---

## Hook (First 3 Seconds)

> **"Aprel oyi. 200 ta ish. 1 hafta."**
> ("April. 200 papers. 1 week.")

Text overlay on a dramatic visual: towering stack of handwritten papers.

## Production Notes

- Lighting contrast matters: "before" dark/cluttered, "after" bright/clean
- Papers in opening shot can be real or styled — no identifiable student work
- Teacher actor should be relatable: 30s–40s, professional clothing
- Keep transitions fast but not jarring — this is about ease, not flash
- Aspect ratio: 9:16 (vertical Reels format)
- End frame: hold on logo/URL for 2 seconds
