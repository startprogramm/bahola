# Reel 27 — New Year AI Assistant

## Hook (0–2s)
Teacher looks directly to camera, slight smile. Speaks: **"Bu yil mening yordamchim bor."** / **"В этом году у меня есть помощник."**

## Visual Sequence (2–28s)

- **2–6s:** Teacher stands in classroom — casual, confident. Addresses camera directly: *"O'tgan yil baholash uchun soatlab vaqt sarfladim. Bu yil esa..."* Trails off, holds up phone.
- **6–10s:** Teacher photographs a student's worksheet with Bahola open. Close-up on phone: OCR extraction animates, text populates in real time. **On-screen text:** *"1-qadam: Suratga ol."* / *"Шаг 1: Сфотографируй."*
- **10–14s:** Grade result flashes onto screen — score, subject breakdown, individual question marks. Teacher nods approvingly. **On-screen text:** *"2-qadam: Natijani ko'r."* / *"Шаг 2: Смотри результат."*
- **14–18s:** Teacher packs bag, grabs jacket, walks toward classroom door — clock on wall shows 3:45 PM. Relaxed, unhurried. **On-screen text:** *"3-qadam: Erta uyga ket."* / *"Шаг 3: Уходи пораньше."* Small emoji: 😄
- **18–22s:** Back to teacher facing camera: *"Bahola — mening yangi yordamchim. Sizning ham bo'lishi mumkin."* Warm, genuine smile.
- **22–26s:** Bahola app homescreen shown briefly. **On-screen text:** *"Bepul. Hoziroq urinib ko'ring."* / *"Бесплатно. Попробуйте сейчас."*
- **26–28s:** Bahola logo. Fade out.

## Voiceover (Uzbek)
> "Bu yil mening yordamchim bor — Bahola. Bir daqiqada baholash, ko'proq vaqt o'zingiz uchun."

## Audio
Warm acoustic guitar — light and uplifting. Calm but energizing. Volume: 50% under voiceover, 70% during walk-out moment. No heavy beats — this is a personal, testimonial tone.

## Duration
~28 seconds

## Notes
- Teacher must feel like a real person, not an actor — direct-to-camera style, natural delivery.
- The 3-step visual structure (photograph → result → leave early) should be clear and punchy.
- Walk-out-early moment is the emotional payoff — linger on it half a second longer than feels necessary.
- Clock prop is critical for the "leave early" scene — it grounds the time-saving claim in reality.
- Show real Bahola UI; avoid showing student names or real scores in close-ups.
