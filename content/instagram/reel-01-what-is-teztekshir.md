# Reel 1: What is TezTekshir?

## Hook Line
**"Ustoz, yozilgan ishlarni tekshirishga 3 soat sarflaysizmi? Bu vaqt tugadi. ⏱️"**
*(Teacher, do you spend 3 hours checking written work? That time is over.)*

---

| Second | Visual |
|--------|--------|
| 0–3s | Close-up of a teacher's hands buried in a stack of notebooks. Tired energy. Red pen. |

## Script / Voiceover

> **[0–3s]** *(Sounds of papers shuffling)*
> "Har kuni... soatlab tekshiruv."
> *(Every day... hours of checking.)*

> **[3–10s]** "Endi o'quvchi faqat rasmga oladi va yuboradi."
> *(Now the student just takes a photo and submits.)*

> **[10–18s]** "Sun'iy intellekt o'qiydi, baholaydi, izoh yozadi — sekundlar ichida."
> *(AI reads, grades, writes feedback — in seconds.)*

> **[18–27s]** "Siz uchun vaqt tejaldi. O'quvchilar uchun — aniq fikr-mulohaza."
> *(Time saved for you. For students — clear feedback.)*

> **[27–30s]** "TezTekshir. Bepul. Bugun boshlang."
> *(TezTekshir. Free. Start today.)*
