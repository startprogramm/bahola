## Music Direction
High energy at the start (the rush of notifications), then smoothly transitions to a satisfied, calm groove as the submissions get checked off. BPM 110-130 opening, settling to 90-100. Something that captures "controlled chaos turning into triumph."

## Production Notes
- Real notification badges on a real TezTekshir screen — must look authentic
- The swipe-and-result sequence (shots 21-26) should be rapid cut — each swipe is under 1 second on screen
- Show the actual submission count as a live number — makes the impact concrete
- Teacher should look genuinely stressed at the hook, then genuinely relieved at the payoff — the emotional arc is the story
- Subtitle everything — most viewers watch without sound
- The "4 minutes / 18 submissions" stat is the hero claim — make it prominent in the caption and end card
