# Reel 11 — Teacher Testimonial: "I Get My Evenings Back"

**Filename:** `reel-11-teacher-testimonial.md`
**Campaign:** Week 5 — Back-to-School Post-Eid
**Duration:** 30–45 seconds
**Format:** Talking-head testimonial with B-roll cutaways
**Language:** Uzbek (primary) with optional Russian subtitle version

---

## Concept

A real (or realistic composite) teacher speaks directly to camera about how TezTekshir changed their weekly grading routine. The emotional core: **time given back = time spent with students and family**. This is the most powerful social proof format — a peer talking to a peer.

---

## Script / Shot List

**[0:00–0:05] — Hook**
- Visual: Teacher at desk, evening, stack of papers in front of them. Clock on wall reads 8 PM. Tired look.
- Text overlay: **"Kechqurun soat 8. Hali 20 ta ish tekshirilmagan..."**
- Voiceover: "Bu mening har haftalik holim edi."

**[0:05–0:15] — The before**
- Visual: Teacher flips through paper after paper. Quick montage. Pen scribbling. Clock advances — 9 PM, 10 PM.
- Sound: Ticking clock, getting faster
- Text overlay: **"O'qituvchi hayoti — shundaymi?"**

**[0:15–0:25] — The turning point**
- Visual: Teacher pulls out phone. Opens TezTekshir app. Photographs the stack of papers — one by one, fast.
- Screen capture: Upload screen → grading result with score + feedback
- Text overlay: **"Keyin TezTekshirni topdim."**
- Sound: Satisfying notification chime as each result appears

**[0:25–0:37] — The after**
- Visual: Same teacher, same desk — but now clock reads 6:30 PM. Papers gone. Teacher smiles, closes laptop.
- Cut to: Teacher having tea with family at home.
- Or: Teacher arriving early to class, relaxed, prepared.
- Text overlay: **"Endi kechqurunim — o'zimniki."**
- Voiceover: "20 ta ish — 18 daqiqada. Har biri uchun aniq izoh."

**[0:37–0:45] — End card**
- Clean background, TezTekshir logo
- Text: **"O'z vaqtingizni qaytarib oling."**
- Sub-text: "Bepul boshlang"
- CTA graphic: "teztekshir.uz"

---

## Audio / Music

- Begins: slightly melancholic, slow beat (the "before" mood)
- Transitions at 0:15: beat lifts, energy picks up
- End: warm, resolved, satisfying — not triumphant, just peaceful

## Production Notes

- Teacher actor should feel real and relatable — not a model, not over-styled. Real Uzbek school teacher energy.
- The clock progression is the key visual motif — use it deliberately.
- If using a real testimonial, ensure written consent and use first name only.
- The "evening with family" scene adds emotional resonance — shoot warm, golden light.
- Aspect ratio: 9:16 vertical
- Pacing: slow build, fast montage (0:05–0:25), then slow warm resolution
- Keep text overlays minimal and large — legible on mobile
