## Music Direction
Two distinct moods: heavy, tired, slow for the BEFORE section (think lo-fi but melancholy). Then a clean, uplifting shift for the NOW section — hopeful and warm, morning-energy feel. The contrast in music carries the emotional arc even for silent viewers.

## Production Notes
- This reel lives or dies on the casting and lighting contrast — BEFORE must feel visibly tired and dim, NOW must feel fresh and bright
- Same actor (same teacher) in both sections for credibility — real before/after energy, not just a voiceover
- The "28 submissions graded" stat must show on screen — real UI, not a mockup
- The feedback detail shot (shot 37-42) is critical — viewers need to see that the AI feedback is actually specific and useful, not generic
- Caption lists (before/after) are designed for high shareability — teachers will tag each other
- Subtitle everything — most viewers watch without sound
- Consider adding a "❤️ tag a teacher who needs this" CTA in comments to drive shares
