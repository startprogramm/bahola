# Reel 15 — Next Year Pitch

**Filename:** `reel-15-next-year-pitch.md`
**Campaign:** Week 7 — Graduation Season & New School Outreach
**Duration:** ~25 seconds
**Format:** Direct, energetic CTA
**Language:** Uzbek + Russian (overlays)

---

## Concept

A direct, punchy pitch aimed at schools that haven't signed up yet. The visual contrast is physical and immediate: one tap to grade 30 papers vs. a towering stack on a desk. No slow burn — this reel moves fast and ends with a clear call to action. Targets directors and department heads thinking about next year.

---

## Hook (First 3 Seconds)

> **"Kelasi yil ham shunday ishlash istaysizmi?"**
> ("Do you want to work like this again next year?")

Close-up of a massive stack of ungraded papers on a desk. Then: one tap on a phone screen.
