# Reel 75 — Director's Digital Readiness Report (50 Seconds)

## Concept
Speaks directly to school directors about the Uzbekistan 2030 digital mandate. Frames TezTekshir not as a teaching tool, but as a readiness signal — a way to show inspectors, parents, and the ministry that this school is ahead of the curve. Authoritative, confident, aspirational.

## Hook (0–5s)
School hallway. Director walking purposefully.
Bold overlay: **"2030 — raqamli baholash majburiy bo'ladi"**
Subtext (RU): **"2030 — цифровое оценивание станет обязательным"**
Cut to: Ministry document or official-looking slide with "Raqamli O'zbekiston — 2030" heading.

## Scene Breakdown

**[0–7s] — Haqiqat / Реальность**
- Screen: stylized "Raqamli O'zbekiston 2030" reference
- Overlay: "Davlat dasturi — ta'limni raqamlashtirish"
- Overlay (RU): "Государственная программа — цифровизация образования"
- Voiceover (UZ): "O'zbekiston 2030 yilga qadar barcha maktablarda raqamli baholashni joriy etishni rejalashtirmoqda"

**[7–17s] — Muammo / Вопрос**
- Director at desk, looking at paper gradebook
- Overlay: "Sizning maktabingiz tayyor mi?"
- Overlay (RU): "Ваша школа готова?"
- Director turns to camera — direct eye contact
- Voiceover (RU): "Инспекторы уже проверяют: есть ли у школы цифровые инструменты?"

**[17–30s] — TezTekshir — javob / Ответ**
- Screen: TezTekshir director dashboard

- Bold: **"TezTekshir — maktabingiz kelajagi"**

## Production Notes
- Director should be someone with natural authority — not a teacher
- Ministry/government reference: use publicly available "Raqamli O'zbekiston" branding (no fabricated documents)
- Dashboard screen recordings: real TezTekshir director dashboard, anonymized
- Inspector scene: brief and clear — clipboard, tablet handoff, nod of approval
- Tone: confident and aspirational, not fearful — this is an opportunity, not a threat
- Music: authoritative, modern — think "forward-looking institution" energy
- Subtitles in Uzbek and Russian throughout
- End card: emphasize "free for your school" — removes cost barrier for directors
