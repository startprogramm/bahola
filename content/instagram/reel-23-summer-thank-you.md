# Reel 23 — Summer Thank You: This Year, You Graded Smarter. Next Year, Even Smarter.

**Filename:** `reel-23-summer-thank-you.md`
**Campaign:** Week 11 — End of School Year Farewell (2026-05-19)
**Duration:** 30–40 seconds
**Format:** Narrative / emotional — teacher at desk, voiceover-style text, warm tone
**Language:** Uzbek text-on-screen + warm, emotional background music

---

## Concept

A quiet, warm farewell reel. A teacher at their desk on the last day of school. They close their laptop. The moment feels earned — a full year of hard work completed. Text-on-screen delivers the emotional payoff: "This year, you graded smarter. Next year, even smarter." TezTekshir logo fades in gently. No hard sell — just gratitude and a forward-looking promise. This reel is for teachers who gave their whole year to their students.

---

## Hook (First 3 Seconds)

> **"O'quv yilining oxirgi kuni."**
> *(The last day of the school year.)*

Soft, warm visual: a teacher's desk, late afternoon light coming through the window. Teacher sits at desk, reflective.

- Quiet, not dramatic — this is a thank-you, not a climax

- Tempo: slow, around 70–80 BPM

## Production Notes

- Tone: warm, emotional, grateful — this is NOT a sales reel, it's a farewell
- The laptop close is the key visual moment — it should feel earned and satisfying
- Late afternoon golden light is essential for the emotional tone — avoid harsh or bright lighting
- Teacher should look authentic and tired-but-happy — real emotion, not performed
- Text transitions should be slow fades, not sharp cuts — match the reflective mood
- Logo fade-in should be gentle — TezTekshir is a quiet presence, not a loud brand moment
- Aspect ratio: 9:16 (vertical Reels format)
- Pairs with Telegram Post 33 (summer-thank-you) for cross-channel alignment
- This is the emotional anchor of Week 11 — give it room to breathe
