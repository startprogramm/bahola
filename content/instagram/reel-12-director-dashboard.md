# Reel 12 — Director Dashboard: Track Grading Quality Across Your School

**Filename:** `reel-12-director-dashboard.md`
**Campaign:** Week 6 — End-of-Semester Push
**Duration:** 30–45 seconds
**Format:** Screen recording / product walkthrough with text overlays
**Language:** Uzbek (overlays) + English hashtags

---

## Concept

Show the view from a school director's seat: one screen, 20+ teachers, every assessment tracked. The reel walks through the director dashboard — class overview, grading completion rates, submission counts — making the case that TezTekshir isn't just for teachers, it's a management tool for school leaders.

---

## Hook (First 3 Seconds)

> **"20 ta o'qituvchi. 1 ekran. Hammasi nazorat ostida."**
> ("20 teachers. 1 screen. Everything under control.")
