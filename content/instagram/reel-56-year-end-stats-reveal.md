# Reel 56 — Your Semester in Numbers: Stats Reveal

**Campaign:** Week 28 — Year-End Celebration (Dec 14-18, 2026)
**Duration:** 20-25 seconds
**Theme:** Celebrate the semester with real data from the analytics dashboard

---

## Hook Frame (0-2s)

**Text overlay:** "Bu semestr qancha ish qildingiz? 📊"
*(Translation: "How much work did you do this semester?")*
