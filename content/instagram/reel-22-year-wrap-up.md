# Reel 22 — Year Wrap-Up: A Whole Year. Thousands of Assessments. All Graded in Seconds.

**Filename:** `reel-22-year-wrap-up.md`
**Campaign:** Week 11 — End of School Year Celebration (2026-05-15)
**Duration:** 25–35 seconds
**Format:** Montage-style — rapid cuts, text-on-screen, upbeat music
**Language:** Uzbek text-on-screen + upbeat music (no voiceover)

---

## Concept

Montage-style reel that celebrates the school year through a teacher's journey with Bahola. Quick cuts show the full workflow — opening a photo of student work, uploading it, watching the AI grade in seconds. The pace is energetic and celebratory. End card delivers the payoff line with impact. This is a "year in review" moment that makes teachers feel proud of how they worked smarter.

---

## Hook (First 3 Seconds)

> **"Bir yil. Minglab baholashlar."**
> *(A whole year. Thousands of assessments.)*

Bold white text on a warm golden background. Quick cut to a phone screen showing a stack of student work photos.
