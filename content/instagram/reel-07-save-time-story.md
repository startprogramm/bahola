# Reel 07 — Teacher Before/After Efficiency Story

**Platform:** Instagram
**Duration:** 45–60 seconds
**Hook:** "Oldin: 3 soat. Keyin: 20 daqiqa." (Before: 3 hours. After: 20 minutes.)

---

## Script / Shot List

**[0:00–0:04] Hook — Split screen title card**
- Left side (dark/warm): "OLDIN" label
- Right side (bright/cool): "KEYIN" label
- Text fades in: "Baholashda necha soat sarflaysiz?"
- Melancholy music note → transitions to upbeat

**[0:04–0:18] BEFORE side — The struggle**
- Shot: Teacher at kitchen table, 10:30 PM, stack of papers, red pen
- Text overlay: "Kechasi soat 10:30"
- Shot: Teacher rubbing eyes, coffee cup, highlighter, scribbled marks
- Text overlay: "Har bir ish uchun 10 daqiqa..."
- Shot: Clock montage — 11:00 PM, 11:45 PM, midnight
- Text overlay: "3 soat. Har kuni."
- Slow, tired energy. Muted color grade.

**[0:18–0:24] Transition — Discovery moment**
- Teacher's phone lights up with notification / colleague shares TezTekshir link
- Text overlay: "Keyin hamkasbi TezTekshir haqida aytdi..."
- Music picks up tempo

**[0:24–0:40] AFTER side — The transformation**
- Bright, morning energy. Teacher at desk, natural light.
- Shot: Teacher photographs student papers quickly — phone camera
- Text overlay: "Ertasi kuni..."
- Screen recording: uploads to TezTekshir, AI grades appear — 2 minutes
- Text overlay: "2 daqiqada — 30 ta ish baholandi ✅"
- Shot: Teacher closes laptop, grabs coffee, smiles
- Text overlay: "Endi vaqt — o'zingizniki"
- Shot: Teacher at park / reading / with family (life outside work)

**[0:40–0:50] Stats beat**
- Bold text animation on clean background:
  - "Oldin: ~3 soat"
  - "Keyin: 20 daqiqa"
  - "Tejab qolgan vaqt — har hafta: 10+ soat"
- TezTekshir logo pulse

**[0:50–0:58] CTA**
- Teacher looks at camera (or text-only if no talent):
- Text: "Siz ham sinab ko'ring — bepul"
- Logo + URL: "teztekshir.uz"
